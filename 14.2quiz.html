- What is the difference between var and let? With var you can redeclare and reassign, but let will not allow you to redeclare. Let also creates a block scope. - What is the difference between var and const? Const will not allow you to redeclare nor reassign. - What is the difference between let and const? You can reassign with let and with const, you cannot reassign nor redeclare. - What is hoisting? Hoisting pushes variables to the top of the scope they were declared in, before the code is executed.
